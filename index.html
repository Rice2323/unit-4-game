Gem Collector


A random number will be given to you at thestart of each game.


To win you must match that number exactly by clicking on the crystals below.


For each game, each crystal will add a random amount to your total number of crystals collected. These numbers change each game.


That value of of the crystals is hidden from you until you click the crystal.


If you go over by even 1, you lose. Use caution!!
